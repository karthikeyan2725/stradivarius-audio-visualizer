Setup GLFW:
glfw: cmake -S ./installs/glfw -B ./compiled/glfw -G "Unix Makefiles"  
Move generated lib to /lib and /include files from uncompiled to /include

find gl version:
glxinfo | grep "OpenG"

Convert mp3 to wav:
ffmpeg -i ./wav/memphis-trap.mp3 -acodec pcm_s16le -ac 1 -ar 16000 ./wav/memphis-trap.wav
